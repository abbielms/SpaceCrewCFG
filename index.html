Welcome to the Solar System

Summary of our Solar System

We are all citizens of Earth, which is one of eight planets in our Solar System. Our Solar System contains many other bodies, with the most obvious perhaps being our star, the Sun, which accounts for 99.86% of the total content mass in the Solar System.
The Solar System is inside the Milky Way galaxy, which contains over 100 billion other stars with at least one orbiting planet. The Milky Way is inside the universe, which has billions of other galaxies within it.
The scale of the universe might make our humble Solar System seem very small, but the amazing events and qualities of our local planets are certainly astronomical.

The Terrestrial Planets

The four closest planets to the Sun are called the Terrestrial Planets:

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars

The Outer Planets

The four planets furthest from Sun are called the Outer Planets:

  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune
